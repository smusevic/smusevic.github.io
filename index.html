A Little Bit About Us

Zoo-Ed was an idea born out of passion for both animals and education. We believe that the best way to learn about the animal kingdom is to experience handling the animals for yourself.

Our main aim at Zoo-Ed is to rescue and rehabilitate exotic animals. Our founding member David has been rescuing and rehabilitating exotic animals for around 15 years and between us we have and have a wealth of knowledge of all things animal related. We travel to schools, nurseries and other community events treating you to a truly unique handling experience. We guarantee you will be enthralled by our beautiful animals and it wonâ€™t take long before you fall in love with our wee hedgehog Pippa!

Thank you for taking the time to visit our site and would love to hear from any school, community group, organisation or event organiser who would like us to visit and talk about our animals and the work we do.

Meet The Animals

Pippa the Pygemy Hedgehog

We were given Pippa when her owner could no longer had time to care for her. Pippa was very shy when she arrived but thanks to Heather, she is slowly coming round and begining to enjoy human company again.


Seamus the Rabbit

He is around 2 years old and is doing really well since being in our care. Seamus was quite nervous when he arrived (always checking out the escape routes!) but is settling down now and is possibly the sweetest rabbit we've ever met. He is a very gentle rabbit who loves nothing more than sitting around getting cuddles.

Philip the Guinea Pig

Wee Philip is an absolute star! Philip is around one year old and came to us when his owner could no longer care for him. This little piggy demands plenty of love and affection. Oh yeah. And food is pretty high on his agenda too!

Albert The Bearded Dragon

Our Albert is just gorgeous inside and out. Albert was cruelly abandoned outside a pet shop in the east end of the city. Thankfully the pet shop owner found him relatively quickly as he wouldn't have survived long without his heat mat. Such a friendly boy, we were shocked at who would've abandoned this once much loved pet.

Molly The Domestic Rat

Wee Molly is our very own reptile specialist David's pet rat. Molly loves being handled and is especially good with the children out on visits.

Special Occasions

Our children's parties last for one hour and are jam packed with amazing animals, jokes, games and fast, fun animal facts.

We also offer a wedding package to keep the little one's entertained whilst you share a toast with the newly weds.

Comunity Events

Our two hour petting zoo events are a great way to enhance your profile within the local community. We give everyone the chance to handle the animals and our experienced animal handler will also present fun, friendly and interactive demonstrations. We will talk about their species, habitats, diets, and life spans and for younger children we will focus more on movements and sounds, always making sure that the children can see the animals in action.
